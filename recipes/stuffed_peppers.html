Stuffed peppers

Quick and easy recipe that tastes great too!

Ingredients
  • Green peppers
  • Jalapeno
  • Black beans
  • Rice
  • Cumin
Steps
  1. Cook the rice according to the package
  2. Cut the seeds out of the green peppers
  3. Saute jalapenos
  4. add cumin
  5. add beans
  6. mix in rice
  7. stuff the peppers and bake
